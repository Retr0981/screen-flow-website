Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Overview

ScreenFlow is designed with privacy in mind. We believe your recordings belong to you. This extension only records your screen when you explicitly choose to start a recording.

Information We Collect

Guest Mode does not collect or transmit any personal information. Recordings created in Guest Mode are stored locally on your device.

If you choose to create a ScreenFlow account, only the information necessary to provide your account (such as your email address) is collected.

Recordings

Your recordings are never uploaded automatically. Any upload or sharing action requires your explicit permission.

Permissions

ScreenFlow requests Chrome permissions solely to provide screen recording functionality, including recording browser tabs, desktop windows, camera, microphone and saving your settings.

Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your recordings with third parties unless you explicitly request us to do so.

Data Storage

Settings are stored locally within Chrome. Guest recordings remain on your device unless you choose to export or upload them.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to:

support@screenflow.app
